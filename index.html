01  /  About
Most agencies hand the marketer's plan to a developer who doesn't quite get it — and lose two weeks and 30% of the brief in the handoff.

I do both jobs. I plan the funnel, write the copy, build the site, run the ads, and wire up the automations that connect them. The marketer who built the system understands its capabilities. Nothing gets lost in translation because there is no translation.

Since 2020, I've worked with U.S.-based agencies and direct clients across automotive, construction, training, and SaaS — building everything from $50/day Google Ads campaigns that print leads to full GoHighLevel sub-account architectures with dozens of automations firing in the background.

I'm currently a Computer Science senior at Caraga State University. I graduated high school with honors. I work remotely from Carmen, Agusan del Norte, and I'm fluent in English.
